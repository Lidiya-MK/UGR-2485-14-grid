Machine Learning

AI, especially the realm of machine learning, captivates me with its limitless potential to transform our world. Witnessing how AI reshapes industries, from healthcare to finance, fuels my passion to contribute meaningfully in this landscape. For the reader, here is the definition of machine learning and artifical intelligence.

Machine learning
Machine learning is a branch of artificial intelligence (AI) that focuses on creating algorithms and systems that enable computers to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed.
Artifical Intelligence
Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines designed to think, learn, and perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.

I am particularly interested in machine learning because it is the most important aspect to determine how an AI system will function

Stoicism

Stoicism is an ancient philosophy that originated in ancient Greece, later developed in Rome, and remains influential in modern times. It teaches principles and practices aimed at achieving inner tranquility, resilience, and a virtuous life, regardless of external circumstances. I for one want to achieve those things and reading the works of the stoics has helped me become a better person in addition to my belief in God. I entend to study the works of the stoics intensly in my lifetime.
