Coding can be a fun way to spend time and this hobby is special to me because it aids the courses that I am currently taking at AAU. This past summer, I created a quote randomizer. I took the quotes from my favorite authors and put them in a javascript array then wrote the following function to randomize the quotes on a click of a button.
